My Hobbies

Games

The hobby that I spend most of my spare time on is gaming. I like to spend my time playing all sorts of games, often with my friends who share my interests. Some of my favorite games include Undertale, Team Fortress 2 and Path of Exile. My interest in videogames is also part of the reason I chose ICT, because one day I would like to make a game of my own.

Shows

Sometimes I also like to watch shows, mostly anime, in my spare time, though I don't spend as much time on shows as I do on games. Some of my favorite include Gintama, Hunter x Hunter and Fullmetal Alchemist. A lot of my friends have similar tastes in shows, so I enjoy discussing shows with them.
